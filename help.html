Help Center

Quick answers to common questions

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Click "Start Free Trial" on the homepage. You can sign up with email/password or Google. No credit card required.

How long is the free trial?

30 days with full access to all features. After that, you can upgrade to Premium for €15/month.

Can my partners use the platform for free?

Yes! Partners you invite to your project get free access with full editing capabilities within that project. They only need to pay if they want to create their own projects.

Projects

How do I create a project?

Click "New Project" on the dashboard. Fill in the details or use a pre-built template (KA220-HED, KA220-VET, or KA210).

Can I manage multiple projects?

Yes, Premium subscribers can manage unlimited projects. Each project has its own partners, WPs, tasks, and documents.

Work Packages & Activities

How do activities work?

Each Work Package can contain multiple activities (A1, A2, A3...). Each activity has a title, objective, description, responsible partner, and deadline. Mark them complete as you go.

Budget

How does lump-sum tracking work?

Enter your total grant amount and allocate per WP and per partner. The system tracks transfers (pre-financing, interim, final) and alerts you if WP1 management exceeds 20%.

AI Reports

How does AI report generation work?

Click "AI Report Generator" in the sidebar. Select which sections to include, choose a language, and click Generate. The AI analyzes your project data and produces a draft final report.

Can I edit the AI report?

Yes, the report is a starting draft. Copy it, export as PDF, and edit as needed before submission.
