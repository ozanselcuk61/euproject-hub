Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

EUProject Hub ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform at euprojecthub.eu (the "Service").

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) (EU) 2016/679 and all applicable data protection laws.

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

EUProject Hub
Email: privacy@euprojecthub.eu

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Account Information

  • Name, email address, organization name
  • Authentication data (provided via Firebase Authentication)
  • Google account information (if using Google Sign-In)

3.2 Project Data

  • Project details (name, description, dates, budget)
  • Partner organization information
  • Work packages, tasks, deliverables
  • Documents and files you upload
  • Dissemination and meeting records

3.3 Usage Data

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited)
  • Activity timestamps

3.4 Payment Data

Payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. We do not store credit card numbers. See Stripe's Privacy Policy.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Contract performance — Processing necessary to provide the Service (Art. 6(1)(b))
  • Legitimate interests — Analytics, security, service improvement (Art. 6(1)(f))
  • Consent — Marketing communications, cookies (Art. 6(1)(a))
  • Legal obligation — Tax and accounting requirements (Art. 6(1)(c))

5. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Generate AI-powered reports based on your project data
  • Improve and optimize the platform
  • Respond to support requests

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud — Infrastructure and authentication
  • Stripe — Payment processing
  • Google AI (Gemini) — AI report generation (project data sent for processing)

All third-party processors comply with GDPR and have appropriate data processing agreements in place.

7. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Firebase/Google Cloud servers located in the European Union (europe-west1). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days. Some data may be retained for legal compliance for up to 7 years.

9. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — Receive your data in a structured format
  • Restriction — Restrict processing of your data
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — At any time, without affecting prior processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@euprojecthub.eu.

10. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. Firebase may use cookies for analytics.

11. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification.

13. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries:
Email: privacy@euprojecthub.eu

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority.
