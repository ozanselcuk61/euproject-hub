Features

Everything You Need for EU Project Success

Purpose-built tools for Erasmus+ and EU-funded projects of all types

Project Management

Create and manage multiple EU projects with full lifecycle tracking. Support for KA220, KA210, KA15X and more.

Partner Collaboration

Add partners with dual contacts, invite them to collaborate, and manage roles across borders in real-time.

Work Packages & Activities

Organize WPs with activities (A1, A2, A3...), set objectives, deadlines, and track completion per activity.

Lump-Sum Budget

Full lump-sum budget tracking with per-WP and per-partner allocation, transfer recording, and 20% management rule alerts.

Smart Task Management

Kanban board, list and calendar views. Filter by WP, assignee, priority. Deadline reminders and overdue alerts.

AI Final Reports

Generate comprehensive final reports with AI. Auto-compile WP outputs, budget summaries, and dissemination impact.

Gantt Chart & Timeline

Visual WP timeline with activity bars, progress tracking, and current month indicator. Export-ready project overview.

Document Management

Organized folders, drag & drop upload, download links. All files stored securely in the cloud.

Dissemination Tracking

Log events, publications, social media reach. Track multiplier events and generate dissemination reports.

TPM & Meeting Planner

Plan transnational meetings with agendas, locations, and attendee management. Track completed and upcoming TPMs.

Dark Mode & Multi-language

Switch between light and dark themes. Available in English, Turkish, German, French, and Spanish.

Export & Backup

Export project data as CSV, JSON backup, or PDF reports. Keep your data portable and secure.
