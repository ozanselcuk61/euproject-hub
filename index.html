How It Works

Get Started in Minutes

From sign-up to full project management in three simple steps

1

Create Your Project

Enter your project details — name, programme type (Erasmus+ KA220), duration, and grant agreement number. Set up your work packages and budget structure.

2

Invite Partners

Add partner organizations with their roles (coordinator, partner, associated partner). Each partner gets their own access and workspace.

3

Manage & Report

Track tasks, upload deliverables, log dissemination activities, and when it's time — let AI generate your comprehensive final report.
