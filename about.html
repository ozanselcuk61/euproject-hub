About EUProject Hub

Modern project management for the EU community

Our Mission

EUProject Hub was created to solve a real problem: EU-funded project management is complex, fragmented, and often relies on outdated tools. We believe project coordinators deserve a modern, intuitive platform that understands how EU projects actually work.

What We Do

We provide a comprehensive cloud platform specifically designed for Erasmus+ (KA220, KA210, KA15X) and other EU-funded projects. Our tools cover the entire project lifecycle — from proposal to final report.

  • Work package and activity management with Gantt charts
  • Lump-sum budget tracking with EU compliance alerts
  • Partner collaboration across borders
  • AI-powered final report generation
  • Document management and dissemination tracking

Our Values

Simplicity: Complex processes, simple interface. No training needed.

Compliance: Built around EU rules and lump-sum model requirements.

Collaboration: Partners join for free, work together seamlessly.

Innovation: AI reports, Gantt charts, dark mode — always improving.
